Cat Facts

Fact 1: Cats Sleep a Lot

Cats sleep for an average of 12-16 hours a day. Some cats can even sleep up to 20 hours in a 24-hour period!

Fact 2: Whisker Fatigue

Cats can experience "whisker fatigue" when their sensitive whiskers come into contact with objects too often, causing stress.

Fact 3: Purring Mystery

While purring is often associated with contentment, cats also purr when they are in pain or anxious.

Fact 4: Rough Tongues

Cats have rough tongues covered in tiny, backward-facing barbs called papillae, which help them groom and clean their fur.

Fact 5: Communication Through Tails

Cats communicate their emotions through their tails. A twitching tail can indicate excitement or irritation.
