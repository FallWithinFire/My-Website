Me!

I'm Kristiana Fernandes and I'm from Northern California. Since I was 15, I've expressed myself on online platforms. I started on YouNow, forming close friendships. In 2018, I moved to Twitch, overcoming challenges to bring consistent, joyful content. My goal is to bring smiles and positivity, making a positive impact.

My Web Development Journey

Web development has always been something I wanted to dip my toes into but didn't know necessarily know how to do it! I've played around with a bit of coding in the past with little tests and things I've tried making for my Twitch channel. I was fortunate to come across Web Development while browsing what Amazon's Career Choice had to offer and had to give it a go, even if it meant stepping outside of my comfort zone because school environments have never been nice to my social anxiety. Since then I've learned a lot of useful information and even took it a step further and did a bit of research on my own and nothing compares to the satisfaction of building a code and watching your vision come to life!
