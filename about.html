About Me

Content Creator

My name is Kristiana Fernandes and I'm from Northern California. Since the age of 15, I have actively engaged in self-expression through online platforms. My journey began on YouNow, where I forged meaningful connections with some of my closest friends. In 2018, I transitioned to Twitch, despite facing several challenges that impeded the growth of my platform. However, I take pride in maintaining consistency in delivering content that brings joy and positivity to my audience. My ultimate goal is to evoke smiles and make a positive impact on people's lives. While I consider myself a rather reserved individual in person, the online space provides a platform where I can be my authentic self and feel a sense of purpose in serving my community.


My Goals

  • Improve my art skills
  • Network with other content creators
  • Find a healthy balance between work and personal life
